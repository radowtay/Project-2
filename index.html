Texas Registered Sex Offender Data:


Buying a house is one of the largest purchases that someone makes in their lifetime. People look at a variety of factors from price, size, location, school zones, and even crime rate. Our project is focused on the crime rate factors and specifically, crimes of a sexual nature. This new database can be used by prosepctive home buyers and even realtors, to help with selecting a new home or location to live in.

Our database looks to compile this information in an easier way for realtors and families to evaluate the county that they would like to live, in regard to sexual assault and related crimes when moving to Texas.
